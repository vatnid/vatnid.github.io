Check your language knowledge!

Not sure how good you are? Want a challenge?
Want to know how competent you are at English?

This is a simple game that helps you find out
how good you are at English vocabulary.


Here's a great way to learn new words and review those you already know.

Whenever you are ready, click on "PRACTISE MODE" to work on some exercises before you move onto the actual test.

Click on "CHALLENGE MODE" to start the level test.


The test consists of 4 levels:

Level 1 - BEGINNERS: Start with simple words and familiarize yourself with the rules!

Level 2 - INTERMEDIATE: Test yourself with more advanced words!

Level 3 - ADVANCED: Try beat an average school student!

Level 4 - EXPERT: Upon completion of this level, call yourself an English pro!


In each level, you will see words falling on the screen.
All you need to do is write down the corresponding English words in the blank box and press SUBMIT.



Don't forget to click the "Submit" button before you click on "Next" !
